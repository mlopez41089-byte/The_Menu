About Project

The purpose of this website is to help eliminate food waste and reduce overall grocery cost by creating a site that provides individuals with meal ideas for the whole week utilizing as many of the same ingredients as possible. The website will allow the user to select their choice in cuisine or ingredient to center their weekly meals around then provide a Grocery list and different recipes for each day utilizing as many of the same ingredients as possible.
